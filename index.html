Conway's Game of Life

So, What's Going On Here?

  • We have two types of square, alive and dead
  • When you click the play button, the board determined which squares are alive for the next generation by the following rules:
    • Alive squares with less than 2 neighbors die, as if by underpopulation
    • Alive squares with more than 3 neighbors die, as if by overpopulation
    • Dead squares with exactly 3 neighbors come to life, as if by reproduction
    • All other squares keep their present status for the next generation
  • Clicking a square switches it from alive to dead or dead to alive
  • Pressing the clear button sets all squares on the board to dead
